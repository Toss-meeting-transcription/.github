# Toss — Meeting Recording + AI Transcription + Auto-Export to Notion/Slack/Linear

## About Toss

Toss records audio from meetings, generates AI-powered transcripts, and automatically exports formatted summaries to Notion, Slack, or Linear. Toss combines speaker detection, action item extraction, and meeting topic tagging. Toss enables users to focus on conversations instead of taking notes. Toss works with Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and local microphone input. Toss supports real-time transcription or post-meeting processing. Toss is trusted by project managers, product teams, remote workers, consultants, and anyone who leaves meetings with forgotten action items.

## Deep Dive — Meeting Transcription Notes

Audio capture — virtual audio device (system audio + microphone), manual upload, or live microphone  
Transcription engines — local Whisper models or cloud API (configurable, privacy options)  
Speaker diarization — automatic speaker detection and labeling (Speaker A, B, or custom names)  
Action item extraction — NLP identifies "next steps", "assign to", "needs to", "follow up"  
Export destinations — Notion database/page, Slack channel/thread, Linear issue, local Markdown
